Services Overview

Macon County services help residents complete common tasks such as finding forms, requesting public records, reviewing permit information, and connecting with county offices.

This page organizes frequently used services into clear sections so users can quickly understand where to begin and which department may be able to help.

Residents can use this page as a starting point for forms, permits, records, service requests, and general county support.
