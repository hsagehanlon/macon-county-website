About Macon County

Macon County is dedicated to serving the needs of residents, businesses, and visitors through public services, community programs, and local government resources.

County departments support public safety, planning and zoning, environmental health, infrastructure maintenance, records, and administrative services.

Residents can use this website to access department information, review public notices, find forms and permits, explore employment opportunities, and stay informed about meetings and community events.

Macon County values transparency, accessibility, and clear communication so visitors can quickly find the information they need from any device.
