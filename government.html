County Government Overview

Macon County government provides leadership and oversight for public services, infrastructure, planning, and community programs that support residents, businesses, and visitors.

Elected officials and county departments work together to ensure services are delivered efficiently, public resources are managed responsibly, and community needs are addressed through planning and policy decisions.

Residents can stay informed by reviewing meeting schedules, public notices, and board activities, all of which are designed to promote transparency and encourage community participation.
